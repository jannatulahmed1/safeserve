Understanding Cross-Contamination

Even if a dish does not include allergens as ingredients, it can become unsafe through cross-contact during preparation. Always ask restaurants:

  • Do you use shared cooking surfaces (grills, fryers)?
  • Are utensils cleaned separately for allergy orders?
  • Can my food be prepared in a clean area?

Dining Tips for Allergy Safety

  • Always disclose your allergies to restaurant staff before ordering.
  • Use allergy cards (printed or on your phone) to list allergens clearly.
  • Call ahead and speak with a manager or chef if you're unsure.
  • Review online menus and allergy policies when available.
  • Avoid buffets or salad bars unless strict protocols are followed.
