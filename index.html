Frequently Asked Questions

What is SafeServe? +
SafeServe is a platform designed to help individuals with food allergies find safe dining options.
How can I search for allergy-friendly restaurants? +
You can use the filters on the Restaurants page to choose your allergens, cuisines, and diet preferences. SafeServe will then show restaurants that match your selections.
Can I leave reviews for restaurants? +
Yes, after logging in you can share your experiences and read others' reviews.
Do I need an account to use SafeServe? +
No account is needed to browse, but creating one allows you to save preferences and leave reviews.
How do you verify restaurant allergy info? +
We rely on user feedback and restaurant-provided data, but always advise users to confirm with the staff directly.
